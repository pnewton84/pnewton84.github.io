Save the world with Henry!

Hi, my name is Henry and I am 6 years old. Why do think I have made a website? It's because I want people to stop doing naughty stuff to damage the world.

Warning: Litter

People are dropping litter. If they keep on going the whole world would be a rubbish tip. Please can you help to stop them because they are damaging the wildlife.

Put the litter in the bin or take it home with you. If you see any litter, please pick it up and put it in the bin.

Oil spills

Oil can harm wildlife because sea birds can get it on their wings and they can't fly.

Rubbish in the sea

Rubbish can harm lots of animals and they can't escape. Bottles, plastic ring things, bags, tins and nets that end up in the sea trap sea creatures like turtles, fishes and coral. They get stuck and injured. Birds too can get strangled with the plastic ring things.

Please can you help protect the sea creatures by picking up litter near the sea. You can put things in recycling too.

Don't drop litter

Take you litter home or put it in the bin.

Recycle

You can recycle plastic bottles, cardboard, paper, tins and glass jars.

Composting

You can put things in the compost bin too. Orange peel, egg shells, potato skins, carrot peel, apple peel, strawberry tops.

Care for birds

Put seeds and water out for birds.

Care for wildlife

Mow your lawn less. Grow wild flowers as they are wildlifes favourites.

Bottles

You can use reusable drinks bottles.

Nappies and wipes

You can use reuseable nappies and wipes. You can wash them and put them outside to dry.

Bug hotel

Build a bug hotel with things that bugs like. They like straw, flowers, pine cones, sticks, logs, mud.

Old toys

Send your old toys that you don't like to the charity shop.

Use less water

Turn off your tap when brushing your teeth.

Use less electricity

Don't watch TV lots. Turn off the TV, mobile phone or lights when you leave the room.

Beware: Coronavirus

Just a reminder. Wash your hands please for 20 seconds and please don't go near people that don't live with you.
