FIXED: Modern Warfare, "The store is currently not available"

How I downloaded the DLC without having to signup to Xbox Live or reinstall the full game.

By Paul Newton, February 2021

I bought Modern Warfare for my Xbox One S last week, and after installing from the disc and downloading Data Pack 1, I turned it off for the night.

The next day I tried to start the campaign, but it told me that I was missing further DLC.

The game then tried to open the store, but failed with the error message “The store is currently not available”.

After lots of Googling, the two possible fixes I found were to either uninstall the game and start over, or subscribe to Xbox Live / Xbox Game Pass to then access the DLC - which I don’t have, and don’t want!

However, here is how I got the DLC:

1) I went to the following URLs, and clicked ‘Get’ for each of the following (after logging in using my Xbox account details):

  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Compatibility Data Pack 2
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Campaign Pack 1
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Campaign Pack 2
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Multiplayer Pack 1
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Multiplayer Pack 2
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Multiplayer Pack 3
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Special Ops Pack 1
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare - Special Ops Pack 2

(I got the multiplayer and Spec Ops packs to play offline against bots).

2) Once done, I then went back onto my Xbox One, and on the main home screen with Modern Warfare highlighted, pressed the Menu button on my controller and selected Manage game and add-ons.

3) On the next screen, I clicked the top large link to Call of Duty: Modern Warfare.

4) The next screen should show you all of the DLCs you got in step 1 above. I ticked the checkbox next to each one, and selected 'Save changes' (button on the right of screen).

5) When you press B to exit the menu, the DLC will all start to download to your Xbox.

6) After it has installed, start the game and you will be good to go!
