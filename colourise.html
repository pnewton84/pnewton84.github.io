Colourising WW2 photos from the Far East

My grandad didn’t keep many memorabilia from the war - just his medals and ribbons, and a handful of black and white photos.

For VJ75 I set about colourising his photos. I love the way that adding colour to old photos brings them back to life, and highlights all the tiny details that are often missed in black and white.

I colourised the photos using the website colourise.sg, and made adjustments in Photoshop. Unfortunately, the site is no longer active (although there is talk of the code being made available), but there are plenty of alternatives (mostly paid services), and there is a great mobile app called 'Colorize - Color to Old Photos' which is also worth a look, which comes with a free five-photo trial.

The code which enables the photo comparison below is by MarioDesigns on CodePen.

How to use: Click and drag the slider on each photo left and right to compare before and after.
